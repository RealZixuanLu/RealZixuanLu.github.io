Serial Coders

LA Based Electronic Punk Band

About Us

We are the Serial Coders. We are an Electronic Punk band focusing on adding synthesizers to hard-hitting punk music for heavier breakdowns and cooler mosh pits. We are a party-provoking force on the LA underground, capable of kicking up a riot with the raw-edged squall of a synth or the sharp-elbowed jerk of a guitar. We are in the middle of recording our 2nd full LP, with plans to be released early 2027. Check out our hit single "HTML Savior"

The Band

Meet John

John is a fast-paced synth player who takes heavy inspiration from Massive Attack. The synth cuts above the band nicely to keep the crowd engaged in the music. He keeps everyone dancing all night long!

Meet Zixuan

Zixuan keeps the band locked in time while everyone is moshing. The drums are clear and precise in the recordings, and give us the punchy sound we want!!
